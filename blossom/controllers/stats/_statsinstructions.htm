Stats Component

The stats component is tightly related to the experience component. In order for stats to work, an instance of experience must be configured in the greenhouse backend. A backend instance of stats must also be configured in greenhouse.
Greenhouse-->Blossom-->Stats-->New Stats Instance

Fill out the form accordingly:

If these instances are not configured and the component is added onto a page, automatic instances of these components will be created on the fly, but the instructor must then configure them from the LTI inside Canvas.

There are four modules in the Stats component:

Pace

Pace shows bonuses and penalties --a measure of how well students are keeping up with the red line as it crosses milestones. It also shows what their potential penalty points are if they cross the next milestone, and the potential bonuses they could earn by crossing the next milestone.
Bonuses are green, and penalties are red. Potential bonuses and penalties are also green red, but are slightly grayed out.
Locked in bonus and penalties use a lock icon , whereas potential bonus/penalties are denoted by a clock icon .

Health

Health shows students' current, locked-in penalty/bonus balance. If the overall balance is positive, the bar will be green. If the balance is negative, the bar will be red.

Gap

Gap shows how many points students are ahead or behind the red line. If they are ahead of the line, the bar will be green. If they are behind of the line, the bar will be red.

Stamina

Stamina is a measure of the quality of students' work. If their work quality is low, they will "run out of gas" early and not be able to reach higher milestones because they will run out of points to earn. Students can select to view the percent of points earned for all assignments they have completed so far, or just the last 10 assignments.
