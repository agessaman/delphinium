Easter Eggs Component

Using the component:

Press "H" and "A" at the same time to do the Harlem Shake

Press "R" and "I" to ripple the page

Press "A" and "S" to play asteroids

Press "K" and "A" to play katamari

Press "B" and "O" to play bombs

Press "P" and "O" to play ponies

Press "M" and "Y" to play ponies
